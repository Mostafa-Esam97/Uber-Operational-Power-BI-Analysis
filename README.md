# Uber Data Analysis: Operational Performance & Insights

## 📊 Project Overview
This project provides a deep-dive analysis into **Uber's operational data**, focusing on ride performance, booking status, and revenue trends. The goal is to identify patterns in ride cancellations and customer behavior to improve overall service efficiency and driver-customer rates.

## 🚀 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Rides Analysis:** Analyzed a dataset of **149K total rides** and **149K unique customers**.
* **Ride Success Rate:** Achieved a **62.21% completion rate** across all bookings.
* **Service Quality:** Maintained an average **Driver Rating of 4.23** and a **Customer Rating of 4.40**.
* **Revenue Metrics:** Tracked "Fare of Completed" rides, peaking at over **$8.8M** in high-demand months.

## 🛠️ Technical Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Processed booking statuses including "Completed", "Cancelled by Customer", "Cancelled by Driver", and "No Driver Found".
* Standardized time-based data to analyze performance by day and month.

### 2. Operational Insights & Visualization
* **Booking Trends:** Visualized monthly ride distributions, showing consistent demand with peaks reaching **12.8K rides per month**.
* **Cancellation Analysis:** Detailed breakdown of cancellation reasons, highlighting that **56.25% of cancellations** are driven by customer-related factors.
* **Payment Preferences:** Analyzed payment methods where **Cash (44.98%)** and **UPI** remain dominant.
* **Vehicle Segment Performance:** Evaluated revenue and ride counts across different categories like **Uber XL, Premier Sedan, Go Sedan, and Bike**.

### 3. Advanced Filtering
* Implemented dynamic filters for **Booking Status, Vehicle Type, and Timeframes** to allow for granular operational audits.

## 🧰 Tools Used
* **Power BI Desktop:** For advanced data visualization and dashboard orchestration.
* **Power Query:** For data cleaning and handling booking status logic.
* **DAX:** Used for calculating ride success percentages and average ratings.

---

### 💡 Business Impact
By analyzing the **Cancellation Reasons** (e.g., "Driver not moving towards pickup" or "Change of plans"), this dashboard helps operations managers pinpoint exactly where revenue is being lost and how to improve driver-partner retention and customer satisfaction.
